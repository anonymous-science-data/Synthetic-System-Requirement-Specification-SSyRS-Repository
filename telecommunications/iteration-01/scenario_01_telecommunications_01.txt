Scenario for Telecommunications Sector
1. System Overview
System Purpose:
To provide a robust and scalable telecommunications platform that enables seamless communication across voice, video, and messaging channels. The system aims to optimize network operations, enhance user experience, and offer innovative services like 5G connectivity and IoT integration.

Domain/Context:
Telecommunications, focusing on mobile network operators, internet service providers, and enterprise communication solutions.

Stakeholders:

Mobile and broadband customers (residential and enterprise).
Network administrators and IT operations teams.
Telecommunications regulators and compliance bodies.
Business executives and marketing teams.
User Base Characteristics:

Size: Tens of millions of users globally.
Diversity: Includes end-users of all demographics, business clients, and government agencies.
Geographic Distribution: Global presence, with emphasis on urban, suburban, and rural connectivity.
Roles: Users include consumers, enterprise IT managers, support staff, and system administrators.
2. Functional Requirements
Core Features:

Real-time voice and video communication.
High-speed internet access with adaptive bandwidth allocation.
Centralized customer management portal for billing and support.
Network monitoring and diagnostics tools.
End-to-end encryption for secure communication.
Support for emerging technologies like 5G and IoT devices.
Frequency of Authentication:

Daily for most consumer users (e.g., app login).
Per session or per critical action (e.g., billing updates or configuration changes) for administrators.
Sensitivity of Actions:

High Sensitivity: User data updates, financial transactions, and network configuration.
Moderate Sensitivity: Accessing personal usage history or non-critical settings.
Low Sensitivity: Viewing promotional content or non-personalized information.
3. Non-Functional Requirements
Performance:

Voice and video communication latency under 100ms.
Internet bandwidth up to 1 Gbps for premium users.
Downtime less than 0.01% annually.
Scalability:

Ability to support a 10x increase in concurrent users during peak events.
On-demand expansion of infrastructure for regional growth.
Reliability:

99.999% uptime (five nines).
Automated failover and disaster recovery systems.
Security:

Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for administrators and critical user actions.
Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations.
Real-time threat detection and mitigation.
Usability:

Intuitive interfaces for users with varying technical expertise.
Support for accessibility standards (e.g., WCAG 2.1).
Multilingual user support.
4. Constraints
Technical Constraints:

Legacy systems integration for existing 4G infrastructure.
Dependency on high-speed fiber-optic backhaul for certain regions.
Compliance Requirements:

Adherence to data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
Fulfillment of telecommunications-specific mandates like lawful intercept.
Resource Constraints:

Limited budget for initial deployment in rural and underserved areas.
Staffing shortages in specialized roles such as network engineers and data analysts.
Integration Needs:

Interoperability with third-party billing systems.
Seamless integration with customer relationship management (CRM) platforms and mobile apps.
